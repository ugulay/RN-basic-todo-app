Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using Basic TODO List. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our open-source to-do list application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you may share with us.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Open Source

The App is open-source, and its source code is available for review and contribution on [[GitHub/Source]](https://github.com/ugulay/RN-basic-todo-app). This transparency ensures that you can verify our claims regarding data privacy and security.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it. We recommend using the security features of your device (such as encryption, screen lock, etc.) to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that could collect your data. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking services integrated into the App.
